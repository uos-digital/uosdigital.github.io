5 things you need to know about your SU
  1. Our Students’ Union (SU) is an independent charity, run by student officers elected by students. You’ll automatically become a member when you arrive.
  2. Our SU is your place to get support, discover something new or make a change in the world.
  3. You’ll find over 300 student-led societies - more than 11,000 students were members of our societies in 2024! Across the SU, there are loads of students working to represent your views and lived experiences.
  4. Your SU is home to lots of places to eat and drink, support services and venues which host weekly nights out, live music, comedy and unique one-off events. We host cultural celebrations and global food festivals, so you can explore the world from within our SU.
  5. We have a zero-waste store and a clothes swap shop, where you can refill your groceries or refresh your wardrobe to help to reduce waste on campus.
Daisy Watson
Students' Union President
