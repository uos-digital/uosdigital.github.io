What it’s like to study at Sheffield

You can’t take in a whole city in one visit, but you can certainly get a feel for what a place is like. That’s why we recommend all our prospective postgraduates come to either a virtual or in-person event.

Open days

You need to experience more than just your course description, so please register to attend one of our postgraduate open days. There’s a choice of dates and you can attend either online or in person — or both!

Listen to talks from academics and student panels. See our teaching and learning facilities. Have your questions answered about accommodation, careers, postgraduate finance, applications and more.
