Career progression

Future-focused from day one

Sheffield’s career support system is one of the best in the country. (No 2 in the UK according to the Student Crowd Awards 2023-2024). From the moment your course begins, you’ll be developing into the kind of top candidate employers are looking for.

Whatever stage of career planning you’re at, you’ll find a wide range of job opportunities, events and online resources to help develop your skills, build a network of contacts and hone your employability.

93% of postgraduate taught graduates are in employment or further study 15 months after finishing their studies

Graduate Outcomes Survey 2021/22

Whether you’re looking for work in the UK, your home country or elsewhere, we’re here to help.

You’ll have access to Career Connect, our University’s careers and employability platform, to help you find thousands of job opportunities and secure your next part-time job, internship, or graduate job.

As a Sheffield graduate, you’ll join our global network of over 320,000 alumni. Throughout your degree and after graduation, you’ll also have access to Sheffield Connect, which allows you to connect with Sheffield students and alumni, access mentorship opportunities, and build your professional network.
