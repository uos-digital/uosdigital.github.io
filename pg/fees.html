Fees, funding and scholarships

Funding for home students

A postgraduate masters loan can be secured for the majority of postgraduate taught courses in England.

The amount you get is set at a fixed amount depending on the year your course starts. It is not based on your income or your family’s. If eligible, the loan is paid directly to you. If your course lasts for more than a year, the loan will be divided equally across each year of your course.

It’s then up to you how you choose to spend this. For example, you can use the loan towards paying tuition fees or your living costs.

Find out more information at gov.uk/masters-loan

Loans for Northern Irish, Scottish and Welsh residents

These areas of the UK also offer postgraduate loans. Search on the Student Awards Agency Scotland, Student Finance Northern Ireland or Wales websites for more information.

Alumni discount of 20% up to £2,500

A discount on your tuition fees is available to anyone who’s graduated from the University of Sheffield, or attended a Study Abroad/Erasmus programme at the University.

Tick the ‘UG at Sheffield’ box on the financial declaration page when completing your online pre-registration tasks.
